Rapid Rush Rafting

At Rapid Rush Rafting, our mission is to connect people with the raw power and beauty of nature through safe, thrilling white water adventures. Whether you're a first-timer or a seasoned paddler, we guide you through an unforgettable journey on the river.

History

Rapid Rush Rafting was founded in 2005 by a group of passionate river guides who believed that everyone deserves the chance to experience the thrill of white water rafting. What started as a small operation with two rafts and a pickup truck has grown into one of the most trusted rafting companies in the region. Over the years, we have guided thousands of guests safely down rivers of all difficulty levels, earning a reputation for exceptional safety standards, knowledgeable guides, and an unbeatable sense of adventure.
