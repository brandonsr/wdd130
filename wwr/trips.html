Gentle Rapids Adventure

Perfect for: Families and beginners

Experience the thrill of white water rafting in a safe, controlled environment. Our gentle rapids offer plenty of excitement without overwhelming new paddlers. Great for team building and family bonding.

Moderate Waves Challenge

Perfect for: Intermediate paddlers

Step up your rafting game with our moderately intense course. You'll navigate Class II-III rapids, learning advanced techniques while enjoying the rush of faster currents and bigger waves.

Duration: 3 hours | Group Size: 4-10 people

Extreme Expert Rapids

Perfect for: Experienced and adventurous paddlers

For the adrenaline junkie! Conquer Class IV-V rapids with our expert guides who have navigated the most challenging waters. This is the ultimate white water experience for serious adventurers only.

Duration: 4 hours | Group Size: 4-6 people
